SHOPRITE

The Shoprite Group of Companies (JSE: SHP), is Africa's largest food retailer.[2] It operates more than 2,934 outlets in 15 countries across Africa and the Indian Ocean Islands.[3] The company's headquarters are in Brackenfell in the Western Cape province of South Africa. Shoprite Holdings Limited is a public company listed on the Johannesburg Stock Exchange, with secondary listings on both the Namibian[4] and Zambian Stock Exchanges.[5] As of 2019, the Shoprite Group employed more than 147,000 people in more than 2,934 stores across 15 African countries.[6]
